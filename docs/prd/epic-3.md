# Epic 3: Multimodal Assessment

Add camera integration capabilities that allow children to photograph their written work for immediate AI analysis, feedback, and progress tracking, creating a complete multimodal learning experience.

## Story 3.1: Camera Integration and Photo Capture

As a child,
I want to easily take photos of my written work,
so that the AI tutor can see and help me with my assignments.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. Camera interface is simple and intuitive for children ages 5-13
2. Photo capture works reliably on tablets and computers with cameras
3. Visual guides help children position work correctly for optimal recognition
4. Multiple photo formats and resolutions are supported
5. Photos are captured with sufficient quality for text and drawing recognition
6. Camera interface provides immediate visual feedback on photo quality
7. Retake functionality allows children to improve photo quality easily

## Story 3.2: Work Analysis and OCR Processing

As a system,
I want to analyze photographed student work accurately,
so that I can provide meaningful feedback on written assignments.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. Optical Character Recognition (OCR) accurately reads handwritten text and numbers
2. Mathematical expressions, equations, and diagrams are recognized and interpreted
3. Drawing and sketch analysis identifies key elements and artistic content
4. Work analysis integrates with ChatGPT for intelligent interpretation and feedback
5. Processing completes within 10 seconds for typical student work photos
6. Analysis results include confidence levels and highlight areas of uncertainty
7. System handles various handwriting styles, pencil darkness, and paper conditions

## Story 3.3: Immediate Feedback and Correction

As a child,
I want to receive instant feedback on my work photos,
so that I can understand mistakes and learn from them immediately.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. ChatGPT analyzes work content and provides specific, constructive feedback
2. Feedback is delivered through both voice and visual annotations on the photo
3. Corrections highlight specific errors and explain the reasoning behind them
4. Positive reinforcement is provided for correct work and good effort
5. Feedback adapts to child's age level with appropriate vocabulary and concepts
6. Suggestions for improvement are actionable and encourage continued learning
7. Feedback integrates with overall learning progress and identifies learning patterns
